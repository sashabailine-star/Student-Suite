How GPA Is Calculated

Step 1
Assignments are grouped into Q1, Q2, Q3, and Q4 for each class.
Step 2
Each quarter average contributes equally to the yearly course average.
Step 3
That yearly course average is converted into base GPA using your custom grade scale.
Step 4
Course level weight is added to calculate the weighted GPA for that class.
Step 5
All yearly course GPAs are averaged into a yearly GPA, then all school years combine into cumulative GPA.
